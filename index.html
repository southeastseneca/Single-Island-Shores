About

Single Island Shores

Single Island Shores is a one-of-a-kind cottage resort on the eastern shore of Seneca Lake in Hector, NY — 29 thoughtfully built vacation rentals spread across a hillside property that reaches all the way to the water. Opened in June 2016 with the first set of lakeside cottages, the property has grown through expansions in 2017 and 2019 into one of the most complete lakeside lodging experiences in the Finger Lakes.

Every cottage features a private deck with panoramic lake views, and the property includes a communal lakefront deck and boat dock, fire pits, gas grills, a children's playground, and free on-site parking with room for boat trailers. Whether you're here for a romantic weekend, a family vacation, a friend group wine weekend, or a quiet work-from-lake escape — Single Island Shores has a cottage to match.

The location is genuinely unbeatable. You're steps from Hazlitt 1852 Vineyards, minutes from 20-plus wineries, breweries, and the Finger Lakes Distilling — and just down the road from Stonecat Cafe, Red Newt Cellars, Lucky Hare Brewing, and Watkins Glen State Park. It's the best possible base for exploring the east shore of Seneca Lake.

Plan Your Stay

Tips for Guests

  • Book the cottage type directly on singleislandshores.com — you can see the map and choose your exact unit and view position.
  • Upper Lakeview cottages have the best sunrise views — Lakefront units are closest to the water for sunsets and dock access.
  • The hill down to the lakefront deck is steep — worth knowing before bringing elderly guests or mobility aids, though accessible units are available.
  • Pick up a s'mores kit at the office when you check in — the fire pits overlooking the lake at night are one of the best parts of the stay.
  • Ask the team for their current winery recommendations — they know the trail and can point you to what's pouring well right now.
  • Two-bedroom and larger cottages include a gas grill with propane — pick up local meats from The Burdett Exchange for a proper lakeside cookout.
